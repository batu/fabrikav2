# <Game> — design brief

> Replace this file's contents when you scaffold a game. It is the shared design
> contract between humans and agents: what the game is, how it should feel, and
> the constraints everyone works within.

## What it is
One paragraph: the mechanic, the fantasy, the session shape.

## Feel
The 3–5 adjectives the game should evoke, and any motion/juice references (link
to `refs/`).

## Constraints
Platform targets, monetization posture, content scope, and anything a
contributor must not break.
