## 5. Evaluation protocol (pixelsmith, rigorous)

Every generated panel and every composited hide crop is judged BEFORE acceptance.
Judge spec (passed via `pixelsmith judge --spec`, reference = the old repo panorama
of the matching style):

1. **Style fidelity** — palette, line quality, light temperature, and material
   richness must match the reference style plate; flag flat-poster drift, plastic
   render drift, or palette inversion. Score /10, floor 7.
2. **Density** — organized chaos: ≥3 depth planes, repeated humanoid-scale prop
   classes, no dead wall >15% of panel width. Floor 7.
3. **Print hygiene** — no gibberish text, no logos, no watermark artifacts,
   no duplicated-object stamping. Hard fail on any.
4. **Seam discipline** — panel edges end on declared architecture (column/arch/
   shelf), band layout within ±40px of the level's band spec. Hard fail otherwise.
5. **Hide fairness (per-hide crops, painted + white + in-context phone crop)** —
   first read must be the disguise object; exactly ONE tell visible at 390pt width;
   no unpainted-sticker read (interior lighting must sit in the style, not on top
   of it). Score /10, floor 7, conductor eyeball is final.
6. **Phone readability** — a 390×844 crop at every zone stop must read its focal
   cluster in <1s glance terms: silhouette separation and value contrast ≥ the
   reference's own phone crops.

Two consecutive rubric failures on the same asset → change approach (reprompt
family, fusion repair, or reassign the hide) — never brute-force re-rolls.

## 6. Production order

1. Style probes: Level 2 panel B in screenprint + gouache (the two strongest
   original verdicts) → judge → pick the production style per level.
2. Full panels per level in the winning style; seams + bands per protocol.
3. Painted hides per roster (magenta-key pipeline), white derivation, per-hide
   judging; local interior+halo fusion where the pasted-read flag trips.
4. Lido (level 1) re-art in the winning style LAST — it must match the saga.
5. Device gate re-run after CAM-7 shell + new levels wire.
